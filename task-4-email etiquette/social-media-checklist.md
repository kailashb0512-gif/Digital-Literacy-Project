
# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

 ✅ Do's
- Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Use professional language, especially when interacting with teachers or recruiters.
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages.
 ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details like phone number, address, or passwords.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
- Do not engage in online arguments or trolling.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files.
- Do not post anything that could harm your reputation or future career.
